Bolognese

Description

A simple Bolognese recipe for excellent chunky pasta sauce with beef, pork, lots of vegetables, and tons of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 4 Slices Bacon
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Pound Ground Pork
  • 28 Ounce Plum Tomatoes
  • 6 Ounces Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 Stalk of Celery
  • 1/2 Cup Dry White Wine
  • 1/2 Cup Chicken Stock
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Dried Basil
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 1 Pound Pasta

Steps

  1. Mince the onion and garlic into their own bowls.
  2. Slice bacon into 1/2 inch pieces.
  3. Slice mushrooms into own bowl.
  4. Shred carrots into own bowl.
  5. Chop celery into own bowl.
  6. Warm oil in a large skillet over medium heat, Saut√© onion, bacon and garlic in the oil until bacon is brown and crisp (about 10 mintues). Use a slotted spoon to transfer bacon mixture to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
  7. Cook ground beef and pork in same skillet until brown and crumbly (5-7 minutes).
  8. Stir bacon mixture, tomatoes, mushrooms, tomato sauce, carrots, celery, wine, stock, basil, oregano, salt and pepper into skillet.
  9. Bring to boil, cover and reduce to simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  10. While simmering, fill large pot of lightly salted water to boil and add pasta and cook until al dente (8-10 minutes), drain once cooked.
  11. Serve sauce over cooked pasta and enjoy!
