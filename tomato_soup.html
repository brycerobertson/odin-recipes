Tomato Soup

Description

This easy tomato soup is my go-to recipe. It's simple and delicious, ready in 30 minutes, and I give leftovers to my kids for lunch. I usually serve it with rosemary bread for dunking.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Thinly Sliced Onion
  • 2 Large crushed garlic cloves
  • 28 Ounces whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 Cup water
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 Tablespoon celery seed
  • 1/4 Teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 Pinch red pepper flakes
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat butter and olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Cook onion and garlic in saucepan until onion is soft and translucent.
  4. Add tomatoes, water, sugar, celery seed, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt & pepper and bring to boil.
  5. Once boiling, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 15 minutes.

  7. Reheat until hot and add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Enjoy!
